Getting started

How to explore the platform

Follow these 4 steps to see everything the platform can do. No technical knowledge needed.

1

Start the servers

Ask Praveen (or any developer) to start the app. They'll run two commands and give you a link — usually localhost:5173. Open it in Chrome or Safari.

One-time setup
2

Browse the public website

You'll land on the marketing home page — the site your prospective members will see. Explore:

→ Branches — see all 5 locations with photos, amenities, seat counts
→ Pricing — plans from hot desks to managed offices
→ Corporate — enterprise solutions page
→ Blog — content marketing with full articles
→ Book a Visit — lead capture form with branch picker

3

Try every role

Click "Sign in" at the top right. You'll see a panel with 8 demo accounts. Click any role card to instantly enter that dashboard. Try all of them!

You can click "Switch role" in the top bar to jump back and try another one.

4

Test the workflows

Inside each dashboard, try real actions:

• Sales exec: drag leads across the pipeline, open a lead, change stage, add notes
• Member: view bookings, invoices, membership details
• Reception: check in visitors, manage walk-ins
• Super admin: see all branches, users, pricing, analytics
